## Dormant Projects

### Needs Proof of Life ✨

> These projects haven't shown recent activity but they're not gone. If you built one of these and are still maintaining it, or want to adopt and revive one, we'd love to hear from you. Open an issue and let's get you back on the map.

> Not sure where to start? Browse the repos below. You might find a contract pattern, a proof structure, or a UI approach that saves you weeks of work. Good code doesn't expire just because the project went quiet.

> Found something useful? Star the repo and open an issue to let the original builder know. They might surprise you.
